    <article class="markdown-body entry-content" itemprop="mainContentOfPage"><h4><a id="user-content-author-of-program" class="anchor" href="#author-of-program" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>Author of program:</h4>

<p>-Yunwei Li</p>

<h4><a id="user-content-date" class="anchor" href="#date" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>Date:</h4>

<h4><a id="user-content-purpose-of-program" class="anchor" href="#purpose-of-program" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>Purpose of program:</h4>

<p>-A simple Pong game (to be played by your right hand against your left hand)</p>

<h4><a id="user-content-development-environment" class="anchor" href="#development-environment" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>Development environment:</h4>

<p>-Processing 2.2.1
-Windows 7 (64bit)</p>

<h4><a id="user-content-launching-instruction-for-windows" class="anchor" href="#launching-instruction-for-windows" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>Launching instruction: (for Windows)</h4>

<ol>
<li>Navigate to ".\Pong\application.windows64" or ".\Pong\application.windows32"</li>
<li>Double click "Pong.exe" to launch the game</li>
</ol>

<h4><a id="user-content-game-instructions" class="anchor" href="#game-instructions" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>Game instructions:</h4>

<p>-"S" and "S" key to control the right paddle</p>

<p>-"O" and "K" key to control the left paddle</p>

<p>-"Space" to start, pause and resume the game</p>
